Here is a handful of videos I've worked on in the past!


What Makes Our School Super?

This video was made for a contest where we had to showcase what made our school super. My focus in this video was originality because the more unique a video is, the more likely it is to be remembered. I did more of a general ad for our school by showcasing a variety of cool things we have at our school. Other people in our class went for a more interview type video and I think that us doing what we did really diversified it and made it a better video as a result!


The Best Speed Game You've Never Played

This is a video I ended up making about a very underrated game I found called, Astropods: Starside Glaze. It got the developers attention and I feel like it spread the game to a lot more people that would've otherwise never found it! This is also the first video I actually uploaded publicly to my Youtube channel so the quality is...not always great.


The Mega Fluff Cup Trailer

I made this video for a Pokemon tournament I hosted at my school! I think it's a pretty decent trailer overall and it clearly conveys everything you need to know about the event. The only thing not said in the video is the tournament date which was October 25th at 12pm.


Lost Voting Commercial 2022/24

I made this for a project in my Civics class! We were tasked with making an advertisement to vote and we had a few different choices on how we do it. My teammates originally wanted to do a slideshow which made me almost make the whole video myself because I'm of the mindset where if I can make a video, I'm going to and if they don't want to help I'll do it myself. We added a bunch of funny things into this video and just had a fun time with it.


DNA Bracelet Commercial

I made this for yet another school project, this time for Biology. We had to make DNA Jewelery and I felt like making an ad for it. So we did and had a lot of fun again! We made this ad much shorter than one that we had done before and I feel like as a result our pacing was much better!
